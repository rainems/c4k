About Crayons4Kids

"It all started with a box."

Crayons4Kids is a 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to brightening the lives of hospitalized children across America. We donate crayons, coloring books, toys, and games to hospitals all over the U.S.

  • 2001

    The Humble Beginnings

    It all began with Emily and Matt's cardboard box on their porch, left out to collect crayons and books for the children in the hospital at which their dad worked. Friends and Neighbors were the first to contribute to the mission.

  • December 2017

    How Far C4K Has Come

    Their reach expanded beyond their local hospital to Kalamazoo, Los Angeles, Boston, and Philadelphia. C4K has collected more than hundreds of thousands of new crayons, coloring books, games and toys.

Operation Scribble

During Operation Scribble, we posted all the "Big Ticket" wishlist items desired from Bronson Children's Hospital. We were able to purchase EVERY SINGLE ITEM on the wishlist including 4 iPads, 2 mamaRoo swings, 2 portable DVD players, a special instant camera, several musical instruments, and much more. This is all in addition to the 95 boxes full of toys collected during Operation Scribble!

Operation Scribble

Annually starting in 2005

Operation Scribble is a 12 hours radio-a-thon with WRKR 107.7. We collect toys games stuffed animals and monetary donations to gear up for Christmas. We have tons of nurses doctors and child life specialists come talk on the radio to talk about the kids and what these gifts do for them. Also past patients come and talk to give perspective. this is one of our biggest events and all these toys are specifically given to the kids and NICU at Bronson Christmas Eve and day.

Christmas Eve and Christmas Day Deliveries

Annually on Christmas Eve starting in 2005

Every child in the hospital will get a huge package to open. Neonatal Intensive Care babies will also receive a very special gift. We'll also be back for the new patients who are admitted to the hospital on Christmas day.
